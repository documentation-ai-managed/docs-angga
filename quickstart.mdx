## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before you begin, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A Google account for faster sign-up
</Callout>

## Create Your Angga Account

Sign up takes under 2 minutes. Follow these steps to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.angga.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Sign-Up Method" icon="user-plus">
    Select **Email** or **Continue with Google**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox and click the verification link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="edit-3">
    Enter your name and set a strong password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, create a space to organize your projects.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, select **New Documentation Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Space" icon="edit">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and choose a description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Settings" icon="settings">
    Set visibility to **Private** or **Public** and add team members if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Enter" icon="check-circle">
    Click **Create** to access your new space.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Project Documents

Populate your space with markdown files.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Markdown Page" icon="file-text">
    Click **New Page** > **Markdown**.

    ````markdown
    ```markdown
    # Welcome to My Project

    This is your first document.

    ## Features
    - Fast setup
    - Team collaboration
    ```
    ````

    Save as `introduction.mdx`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API Reference" icon="code">
    Use the API components for structured docs.

    <ParamField path="spaceId" param-type="string" required="true">
      Your documentation space identifier.
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField header="Authorization" param-type="string" required="true">
      Bearer `YOUR_API_KEY`.
    </ParamField>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
