---
title: Adding a Team Member
description: Invite teammates to your Angga project.
---

## Overview

Share your documentation by inviting others to your project.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your project">
    Open the project where you want to add a member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Team Settings">
    Click the "Team" tab in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite user">
    Enter their email and select a role, then click "Invite".
  </Step>
</Steps>
