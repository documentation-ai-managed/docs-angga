---
title: Cannot Edit a Page
description: Fixing edit issues in Angga.
---

## Symptoms

- Edit button is disabled
- Changes are not saved

## Causes

- No project permission
- Lost internet connection

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your permissions">
    Ensure you are a project collaborator or admin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh connection">
    Reconnect your internet and reload Angga.
  </Step>
</Steps>
